Help / Howto


Before the event
  1. Complete your user information.
  2. Add a vehicle.
  3. Register.
  4. Pay Online, or at the event.
On the day of the event (autox):
  1. Gates open at 8:00am.
  2. Park your vehicle in the paddock.
  3. Go to registration at the event to check-in.
  4. Get your vehicle checked by the tech inspectors in the paddock.
  5. Drivers meeting at 9:30am.
  6. First car off at 10:00am.
  7. Have fun!
